Welcome to the Sustainable Cities Website!


Hello! We are a group of students from the University of Michigan EcoData team who created this website to share sustainability goals and statistics for cities and countries around the world. Our goal is to help people understand where various places stand on combatting climate change and how we can mitigate the effects as much as possible.
