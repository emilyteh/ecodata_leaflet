Heatmaps

Here's a quick guide on how to interpret each heatmap!

The bar to the right shows how the colors relate on a scale from 0 to 1. Since all the data for each metric is normalized, there is no specific unit associated with each color. For each metric, lighter (closer to 1) or darker colors (closer to 0) may indicate that a lighter color is 'better' and a darker color is 'worse' or vice versa. By looking at each heatmap, the colors show how each city or country compares to another for a particular metric.
